Our Mission

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. We partner with restaurants, hotels, and kitchens to rescue surplus food and deliver it to communities in need. Our mission is to create a sustainable and efficient food distribution network that is safe, hygienic, and dignified for everyone involved.

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up your hotel, restaurant, or kitchen. Provide details about your food capacity, type, and pickup availability.

2. Schedule Pickup

Our volunteer network gets notified. A volunteer will be assigned to pick up the surplus food at your specified time.

3. Safe Delivery

Food is transported in hygienic conditions and delivered to partner shelters, orphanages, and community centers.

4. Get Reports

Receive monthly reports on the impact of your contribution, including the amount of food donated and meals served.
