The Global Food Waste Crisis

Food waste is one of the most pressing environmental and social issues of our time. Every year, massive amounts of perfectly good food end up in landfills, representing a tremendous waste of resources, money, and human effort. Understanding this crisis is the first step toward creating meaningful change.

Households (42%)

Most food waste happens at home due to poor planning, over-buying, and improper storage.

Common household waste:
  • Cooking too much food
  • Food going bad before use
  • Throwing away leftovers
  • Impulse buying without planning
