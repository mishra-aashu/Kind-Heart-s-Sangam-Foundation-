The Five Food Groups

Understanding food groups helps create balanced meals that provide all essential nutrients. Each group offers unique nutritional benefits and should be included in daily meals.

üçé

Fruits

Daily Servings: 2-3 cups

Key Nutrients: Vitamins A, C, K; Fiber; Potassium

Benefits: Natural sweetness, antioxidants, hydration

Examples:

  • Apples, bananas, oranges, berries
  • Mangoes, papayas, guavas
  • Citrus fruits, melons
ü•ï

Vegetables

Daily Servings: 3-4 cups

Key Nutrients: Vitamins A, C, K; Fiber; Folate

Benefits: Low calorie, high volume, disease prevention

Examples:

  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale, lettuce)
  • Root vegetables (carrots, potatoes, beets)
  • Cruciferous (broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage)
üåæ

Grains

Daily Servings: 6-8 ounces

Key Nutrients: B vitamins, Iron, Fiber

Benefits: Sustained energy, digestive health

Examples:

  • Whole grains (brown rice, oats, quinoa)
  • Whole wheat bread, roti, chapati
  • Millets (bajra, jowar, ragi)
ü•©

Protein Foods

Daily Servings: 5-6 ounces

Key Nutrients: Protein, Iron, Zinc, B vitamins

Benefits: Tissue building, immune support

Examples:

  • Meat, poultry, fish, eggs
  • Legumes (dal, beans, lentils)
  • Nuts and seeds
ü•õ

Dairy

Daily Servings: 3 cups

Key Nutrients: Calcium, Vitamin D, Protein

Benefits: Bone health, muscle function

Examples:

  • Milk, yogurt, paneer, cheese
  • Fortified plant-based alternatives
  • Traditional dairy products

Lesson Summary

In this lesson, you learned about balanced nutrition:

  • Five Food Groups: Fruits, vegetables, grains, proteins, dairy
  • MyPlate Method: Visual guide for balanced meal proportions
  • Portion Control: Hand method and plate method for appropriate serving sizes
  • Meal Planning: Creating nutritionally balanced daily meal plans

Key Takeaway: Balanced nutrition is about both quality and quantity. Focus on filling half your plate with colorful vegetables, include lean proteins and whole grains, and don't forget fruits and dairy. Use simple visual methods like MyPlate and hand portions to guide your choices and maintain healthy eating habits.
